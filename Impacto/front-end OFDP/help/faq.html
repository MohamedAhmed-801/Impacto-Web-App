OFDP — Food Sharing

Help & Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I request food?

Go to the marketplace, choose the available item, open details, and click “Request Donation”.

2. How do restaurants donate?

Restaurants log into their dashboard and use the “Add Donation” page.

3. How do NGOs accept donations?

NGOs can accept available donations from the “Available Donations” page.

4. What do couriers do?

Couriers are responsible for delivering donations from restaurants to NGOs or end users.

5. How do I contact support?

Use the Contact Us page to send your message.
