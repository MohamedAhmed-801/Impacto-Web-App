Impacto — Online Food Donation

What is IMPACTO?

IMPACTO is a smart food-donation platform designed to connect restaurants, NGOs, and individuals who need support. It helps reduce food waste by redistributing surplus meals in a fast and organized way. The system allows donors to upload available food, and NGOs can instantly request and schedule pickups. Through its clean interface and automated matching process, IMPACTO ensures fair and efficient distribution. Our mission is to build a sustainable community where no good food goes to waste.
